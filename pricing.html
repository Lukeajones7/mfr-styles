Pricing

To trace all your family history (if your parents were born before 1940) – that’s both your parents, all your grandparents and all the lines back to around 1800 is £680.00.

To trace just one side, just your father’s side for example, is just £350.

As well as a folder containing the history of each family line, you also receive a small family tree chart. We do not require full payment in advance, just a £50 deposit and the balance is not due until the work has been completed. If we are unable to trace a line back then we discount the price accordingly.
